Pizza

This is the recipe for an amazing pizza to cook at home.

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • 16 slices of pepperoni
  • 1/2 jar of tomato paste
  • 150 grams of mozerella cheese
  • A pinch of pepper flakes

Cooking Steps!

  1. place dough on work bench
  2. apply tomato paste liberally
  3. sprinkle cheese on top of the sauce
  4. add pepperoni slices
  5. add a pinch of pepper flakes
  6. cook on oven at 200c for 15 minutes
