Pasta

This is the recipe for an amazing pasta to cook at home.

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 11 jar of afraedo sauce
  • 1/2 an onion
  • salt
  • diced bacon

Cooking Steps!

  1. add water & heat saucepan
  2. add spaghetti when boiling
  3. empty water with a strainer
  4. fry onion and bacon in a frying pan/li>
  5. add sauce
  6. turn off heat
  7. add spaghetti to the pan
