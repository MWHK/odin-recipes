Steak

This is the recipe for an amazing steak to cook at home.

Ingredients

  • steak
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1/2 onion

Cooking Steps!

  1. add oil to pan and heat
  2. dice onions and add to pan
  3. add steak and sprinkle with salt and pepper
  4. cook for 3 minutes on each side
  5. remove steak and cover with foil
  6. allow to rest for 10 minutes
